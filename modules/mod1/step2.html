Module 1: The USB Stick

Step 2: Test Bazzite (Try Mode)

⚡ Fast track

When Bazzite boots, you'll see:

→ Try Bazzite (Test without installing)
Select it with arrow keys and press Enter.

Wait 1-2 minutes... Bazzite will start from USB!

🐢 I want to understand

What is "Try Mode"?

Bazzite runs completely from your USB stick. Nothing is installed to your PC yet. It's safe to test:

  • ✅ Check if everything works (WiFi, sound, graphics)
  • ✅ Look around – it's the same as installed Bazzite
  • ✅ Nothing is saved – restart and it's fresh again

First things to test:

  • WiFi: Click top-right, connect
  • Sound: Play a test sound
  • Display: Correct resolution?

✅ Checkpoint

Are you looking at the Bazzite desktop? Everything working?

Common issues:

  • No WiFi? Try wired internet or restart with USB
  • Screen flickers? Might be graphics driver – still safe to install
  • Slow? USB 2.0 is slow – be patient, installation will be faster
  • Stuck on boot? Try the "Basic Graphics Mode" in boot menu
