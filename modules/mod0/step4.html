Module 0: Before the Leap

Step 4: Create Bootable USB

⚡ Fast track

Open Fedora Media Writer and:

  1. Click "Create Live USB"
  2. Select "Bazzite" (or download it)
  3. Choose your USB stick
  4. Click "Write to Disk"
⏳ Wait 5-10 minutes...
Done when it says: "Writing complete!"
🐢 I want to understand

What's happening?

Fedora Media Writer is:

  • Downloading Bazzite (about 3GB)
  • Writing it to your USB stick
  • Making it bootable

If it fails:

  • Try a different USB port (USB 2.0 often works better)
  • Try a different tool (BalenaEtcher)
  • Format USB first (FAT32)

✅ Checkpoint

Do you see "Writing complete"? Perfect! Your USB is ready.

Common issues:

  • USB not detected? Try another port or stick
  • Download too slow? Use direct download and BalenaEtcher
  • Error messages? Take a photo and ask at Campfire
