Module 0: Before the Leap

Step 3: Prepare USB Stick

⚡ Fast track

Download and run Fedora Media Writer:

https://fedoraproject.org/mediawriter/
You need:
  • USB stick (min 8GB)
  • Internet connection
  • About 10 minutes
🐢 I want to understand

Why Fedora Media Writer?

It's the simplest tool to create a bootable USB stick. It downloads Bazzite automatically and writes it to your USB.

Alternative tools:

  • BalenaEtcher – Also very simple
  • Rufus (Windows) – More options
  • dd (Linux) – For experts

Important:

This will ERASE everything on the USB stick. Back it up first if needed.

✅ Checkpoint

Do you have a USB stick ready? At least 8GB, empty?

Common issues:

  • No USB stick? Any old USB works, even slow ones
  • Stick too small? Need at least 8GB
  • Can't download? Try the Bazzite website directly

💾 USB Stick (8GB or larger)
