Module 0: Before the Leap

Step 2: Check Secure Boot

⚡ Fast track

Restart your PC and enter BIOS/UEFI:

Press one of these keys during startup:

F2, Del, F10, or F12

Once in BIOS, look for:

Secure Boot → Disabled
🐢 I want to understand

What is Secure Boot?

Secure Boot checks that your PC only runs trusted software. Bazzite works with Secure Boot, but it's easier to disable it for installation.

How to find it:

  • Different PCs, different BIOS: Look for "Boot" or "Security" tab
  • Setting: Usually "Secure Boot" → "Disabled"
  • Save and exit: Usually F10
Don't worry: You can re-enable Secure Boot after installation. Bazzite supports it!

✅ Checkpoint

Did you disable Secure Boot? If yes, you're ready!

Common issues:

  • Can't enter BIOS? Try mashing F2/Del right after power on
  • Can't find Secure Boot? Look under "Security" or "Authentication"
  • Still stuck? Google your PC model
