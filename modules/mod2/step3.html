Module 2: First Days

Step 3: Install First Apps

⚡ Fast track

Open "Discover" (App Store) from the bottom menu.

Popular apps to install:
🎮 Steam (already installed!)
🌐 Firefox / Chrome / Edge
📦 Discord
🎨 GIMP (Photoshop alternative)

Just search and click "Install".

🐢 I want to understand

How app installation works on Bazzite:

  • Flatpak: Most apps are Flatpaks – sandboxed, safe, updated automatically
  • Discover is the app store that manages Flatpaks
  • Steam comes pre-installed (Bazzite is gaming-focused)

Finding apps:

  • Search in Discover
  • Or visit Flathub.org and click "Install"

Pro tip:

For Windows apps, you can try Bottles or Wine – but that's advanced. Stick to native apps first!

✅ Checkpoint

Did you install at least one app? Does it run?

Common issues:

  • Discover won't open? Restart Bazzite
  • App not found? Try the exact name on Flathub
  • Installation fails? Check internet and free space
  • Need something specific? Ask Bazzi at Campfire
