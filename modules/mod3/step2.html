🐢 I want to understand

Why extra launchers?

Steam is great, but many games are on other stores:

  • Epic Games: Free games every week
  • GOG: DRM-free classics
  • Battle.net: Call of Duty, Diablo, WoW
  • EA App: FIFA, Battlefield

Heroic vs Lutris:

  • Heroic: Simpler, focused on Epic/GOG
  • Lutris: More complex, does everything

Start with Heroic – it's easier. Lutris later if needed.

✅ Checkpoint

Did you install Heroic? Log in and see your games?

Common issues:

  • Heroic can't login? Try logging in through browser
  • Games won't start? Check Wine/Proton settings in Heroic
  • Lutris too complex? Stick to Heroic for now
  • Specific game issues? Ask Bazzi at Campfire
